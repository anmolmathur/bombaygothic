Meet Kruti Garg

*Bombay Gothic was founded in 2021 by Conservation architect Kruti Garg. It combines her love for the city and passion for art and cultural heritage. Her work aims to add art to our everyday lives through a series of relatable illustrated cityscapes of Bombay, evoking a sense of nostalgia and celebrating the unique 'Bombay Gothic' architectural style.*
