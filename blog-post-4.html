Discovering Hidden Details

One of the greatest joys is pointing out a detail that often goes unnoticed: a whimsical gargoyle perched on a university facade, the intricate monkey carvings on the old Secretariat building, or the stunning stained-glass windows hidden within a library. These are the moments when a familiar building suddenly becomes new again. It's the "Indian craftsman's preference to elaboration" coming to life before our very eyes.

"The city reveals its secrets to those who walk its streets with open eyes and a curious heart."

Each walk is a chance to connect with the city's soul. Seeing the look of wonder on someone's face as they truly appreciate the architectural genius around them is why we do what we do. It's a reminder that these buildings are not just structures; they are the keepers of our collective memory, and it's a privilege to share their stories.
